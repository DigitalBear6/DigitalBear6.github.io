Tourist Guide to Johor in Malayisa

Introduction:

    Malaysia is a beautiful country in south Aisa. I lived in ther for about 3 
years, so if you want to visit Johor I have some advices for you.

Hotel & Supermarket & Restaurant:

    I suggest you to chooese Granada hotel. It's in Bukit Indah which is the     
center of Johor.    
    Ther is a Supermarket called AEON near the Hotel. It support basic items    
and snakes. Also, there are many banks and restaurants in the AEON mall.     
Include Chinese traditional foods, Malayisa traditional foods and western   
foods.  
    You can see the AEON mall in your hotel and it takes you about 10 minutes   
to go there on foot. If you want to try some Indian food or find a barber,  
you can go to Haoshichang it provide goods and foods in cheeper price.   
    
    Please aware that the place to sell meat smells really bad.

Interesting facts:

The Indian trditional restaurant are very delicious and 1 million miles away

from dirty, Although most people in there eat foods by hand, but this restaurant
provide fork spoon and and knife.
If you want to eat food by hand, I suggest you
to use belly of fingers and only use you right hand to pretend you know very
well on this.

Conclution:
